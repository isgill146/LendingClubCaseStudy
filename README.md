# Lending Club Case Study
> Perform an analysis to understand the driving factors (or driver variables)
behind loan default, i.e.the variables which are strong indicators of default.  
The company can utilise this knowledge for its portfolio and risk assessment.

## General Information
A consumer finance company specialises in lending various types of loans to urban customers. When the company receives a loan application, the company has to make a decision for loan approval based on the applicantâ€™s profile.
The aim is to identify patterns which indicate if a person is likely to be defaulter, which may be used for taking actions such as denying the loan, reducing the amount of loan, lending (to risky applicants) at a higher interest rate, etc.


## Conclusions
#### Below are the driving factors to identify a defaulter
- Grade
- Interest Rate
- Loan Term
- Home Ownership
- Purpose of Loan
